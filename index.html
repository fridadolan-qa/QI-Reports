📋 Design note: Three alert types, all delivered via Slack. Alerts are sent as summaries, not individual pings per task — graders receive a single daily digest of open/overdue items. Alert 1 = early warning 24h before DSAT deadline. Alert 2 = overdue summary once deadline passes. Alert 3 = manager digest sent the day after cycle close showing all incomplete tasks across the team.
Alert 1 — Early Warning (24h Before Due · To Grader)

Sent directly to the grader 24 hours before a DSAT review is due (72-hour window). Surfaces all items due the following day in a single digest — gives the grader the chance to self-correct before deadline passes and before a manager is looped in.
